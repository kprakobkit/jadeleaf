Jazz Festival 2016

Jade Leaf Eatery and Lounge has been selected by San Jose Jazz to be a venue for the Jazz Festival 2016. There will be numerous famous jazz artists performing on stage at the restaurant on both Winterfest (March 4, 5 and 6, 2015) and Summerfest (August 12, 13 and 14, 2016) festivals. The Annual Jazz Festival in Downtown San Jose attracts thousands of people, and Jade Leaf Eatery and Lounge is very proud and excited to be part of such a great event.
